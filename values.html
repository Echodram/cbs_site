Our Story

Founded in 2005, the Center for Biblical Studies was established to provide rigorous biblical scholarship accessible to all believers. What began as a small study group has grown into an international ministry impacting thousands through our publications, online resources, and educational programs.

2005 - Humble Beginnings

Started as a weekly Bible study group in a local church basement with just 12 members. Our founder, Dr. Michael Johnson, saw the need for deeper biblical understanding among lay believers.

2010 - First Publication

Published our first study guide on the Gospel of John, which quickly became a resource used by churches across the country. This marked our transition to a publishing ministry.

2015 - Digital Expansion

Launched our online platform, making our resources available worldwide. Our first international study tour to Israel connected Scripture with its geographical context.

2020 - Present Day

Now serving over 50,000 subscribers from 120 countries with a team of 12 scholars and educators. Our resources are used in seminaries, churches, and personal study worldwide.

Our Mission & Values

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Biblical Fidelity

We are committed to the authority and sufficiency of Scripture, interpreting it responsibly within its historical and literary contexts.

Academic Excellence

Our team maintains the highest standards of scholarship while making complex theological concepts accessible to all believers.

Practical Application

We bridge the gap between biblical knowledge and Christian living, helping believers apply Scripture to everyday life.

"Our vision is to see every believer equipped with a deep understanding of Scripture that transforms their relationship with God and impacts their communities for Christ."

— Dr. Michael Johnson, Founder & Director
